Off-Sheet Freight Reassignment

If a scanned HWB is currently assigned to another driver/load, the system will show an off-sheet freight warning before allowing any status event to be submitted.

Continuing confirms you are reassigning that freight to your active workflow. This warning behavior is designed to prevent accidental load theft, duplicate handling, or incorrect custody updates.
