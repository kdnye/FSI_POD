General User / Driver Instructions

How to Submit a POD

  • Open New POD from the top navigation.
  • Scan the HWB barcode or type the number manually.
  • Capture the delivery photo and collect the consignee signature on the digital canvas.
  • If you scan an unassigned load, an off-sheet warning will appear and ask for confirmation before reassignment.

Batch Uploads

Use the Upload tab to submit up to 100 scanned documents in one batch. Files are sent to Couchdrop for processing.

Load Board Management

CSV Uploads

Ops/Admin CSV uploads require the following headers:

mawb_number, hwb_number, shipper_address, consignee_address, origin_airport, destination_airport

Use the upload tool to import rows and assign them into the live load workflow.

Use the Live Ops Dashboard to monitor real-time sync and shipment activity.

POD Export
